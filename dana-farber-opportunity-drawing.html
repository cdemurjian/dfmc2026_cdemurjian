Support My Dana-Farber Marathon Challenge Through Our Opportunity Drawing

I'm thrilled to be running the Boston Marathon as part of the Dana-Farber Marathon Challenge, raising funds to support groundbreaking cancer research and patient care. There will be two winners for each prize category, with all prizes valued at $250-300. Whether you're local to the Boston area or supporting from afar, there's a prize tier designed with you in mind!
